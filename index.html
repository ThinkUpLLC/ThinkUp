Understand your networks.

Get in depth tracking of your network's growth and interaction. With ThinkUp you'll get a better understanding of what your fans like without missing a single response.

Take control.

With ThinkUp, your social activity is stored in a database that you control, making it easy to search, sort, analyze, publish and display activity from your network. All you need is a web server that can run a PHP application.

Open Source.

ThinkUp's open source community is very active and always accepting new contributors of all skill levels.

Join us on GitHub today!

ThinkUp is a free, open source web application that captures all your activity on social networks like Twitter, Facebook and Google+. One of the best ways to learn about ThinkUp is to see it in action.

With ThinkUp, you can store your social activity in a database that you control, making it easy to search, sort, analyze, publish and display activity from your network. All you need is a web server that can run a PHP application.

Analyze

ThinkUp gives you insightful graphs & charts in a single simple dashboard to help you make sense of your social network activity.

 

Visualize

Need to see your conversations in a new way? ThinkUp's rich visualizations let you see your replies mapped across the globe.

 

Connect

Browse through all the links & photos shared on your social network, in one click, or easily review any tweets you've favorited.

 

Search

Ever get frustrated that Twitter doesn't let you simply search for your own Tweet from a year ago? ThinkUp's got you covered.

Publish

ThinkUp lets you publish & embed conversations on a blog or website. Everyone can benefit from the wisdom of your social network.

Export

Export your tweets at any time and open them as a spreadsheet in Excel for further analysis. Or just save them for recordkeeping.

Roots

Gina Trapani began building ThinkUp in 2009 as a personal project. Today, our developer community is one of the most inclusive open source projects in the world.

Purpose

Development of ThinkUp is led by Expert Labs, a non-profit startup that helps government tap in to the power of social networks.

From the White House to ordinary citizens, anyone can use ThinkUp to gain insights through crowdsourcing.
