Get better at using your social network.

With ThinkUp you'll get the detailed insights you need to learn what's working, and what you can do even better.

Ready for when an app creeps you out.

Sick of social networks constantly changing their privacy policies and terms of service? With ThinkUp, your stuff is stored in a database that you control, so it's easy to search, sort, analyze, publish and display no matter what changes a service makes.

Open source, but friendly!

ThinkUp's open source community is very active and always accepting new contributors of all skill levels. And ThinkUp is free, both to use and to modify.

Join us on GitHub today!

ThinkUp is a free, open source web application that captures all your activity on social networks like Twitter, Facebook, Google+ and Foursquare. Open Source

 

Community

ThinkUp's vibrant community is growing quickly — and we sincerely welcome newbies! Whether you're an expert or just starting out, your contributions can help make ThinkUp better.

Roots

Gina Trapani began building ThinkUp in 2009 as a personal project. Today, our developer community is one of the most inclusive open source projects in the world.

Purpose

ThinkUp is designed to help everyone get more meaning out of their use of social networks. From the White House to ordinary citizens, anyone can use ThinkUp to gain insights about the time they spend online.
